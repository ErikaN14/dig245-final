About

Sustainability 101 is a website that allows users to reduce their own carbon footprint through a game. When a user opens the page, they are guided to a home screen that asks for your name. This name will then be added on to each user’s own personalized dashboard. On the dashboard, there will be a list of different tasks that the user can complete and a weekly score. For each task that the user completes, their weekly score will be updated by a certain amount depending on how many points a task is worth. After a task is checked off, a new task will appear for the user to complete. All these tasks are meant to reduce the user’s carbon footprint in ways that can easily be implemented in their daily routines. The goal of this website is to show that even the smallest actions taken by a person can have a large impact in our environment. In order to motivate others to take part in this game, there is also a social aspect to this website. Towards the end of the page, there is a social media section. By using the #Sustainability 101, users are able to see how other users are doing with their daily tasks and even use this as motivation to continue with their own tasks and share their own accomplishments with others.
